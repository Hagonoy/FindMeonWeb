Single Doctor Pega Application

The Patient Information System for Single Doctors, which can be used by clinical practitioners, is designed specifically for individual clinics that are typically managed by a single doctor and a secretary. This system's main features are scheduling, consulting, and basic billing.

The scheduling feature is where the patient can book an appointment for their preferred date within the clinic's opening hours. Additionally, they can also view the doctor's unavailable dates.

The consulting feature is where the doctor can list the patient's chief complaints, symptoms, and input rule-outs. In this part, the doctor can also choose between suspicion and a confirmed diagnosis. If the doctor is confident about the patient's condition based on the presented complaints and symptoms, they can select a diagnosis and provide a prescription.

On the other hand, if the doctor needs further information, such as laboratory tests, the doctor can choose the suspect option. This allows the doctor to specify the suspected illness, request necessary laboratory tests, and schedule a return date for a follow-up check-up. Additionally, the doctor can also provide a prescription that can help the illness slow down.
