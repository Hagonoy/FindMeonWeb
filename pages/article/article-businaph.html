Busina PH

January 24, 2024

Introduction

The Busina PH is a system that makes use of QR codes to collect fares uniformly for public transit. The cooperatives can find the registration on the landing page, which is made using React JS and saves their credentials in the database. The cooperatives will be using those credentials upon logging in to the PEGA portal, which will serve as the portal for the operators of the cooperative.

The mobile applications, on the other hand, are divided into two. The conductor mobile application is used by the conductors, who manage the passengers that go in and out of the vehicle. While the passenger application is used by public users, they will be using the mobile app to purchase the tickets or QR codes that the conductor will scan, track their location during the trip, view their transactions as well as their QR lists.
